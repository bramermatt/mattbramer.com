Written Review

oh my my my, this was perfect. This was so so good. This is exactly how any on screen adaptation should be done. From everything from the theme music to the way the show handled the story.. perfect. I loved it! I really think I need to replay the games and review them.. maybe.

Spoilers...

I will be spoiling the show, and also the first game as well. Please be warned.

Sarah

Oh holy cow, Sarah is spectacular! We knew this character that was going to die, and I really enjoyed the way the story delved more into this backstory as we saw in the show.

Nico Parker as Sarah

The Watch

In this show, we got more about this watch that Joel wears, and I loved the way the show portrayed the watch being fixed from Sarah's perspective, because in the game we didn't see that part. But the show just made it so much more impactful and we got the "I sell hardcore drugs" line from Sarah and wow, beautiful. We also got the "your watch is broken" line from Ellie later in the show... ALL THE FEELS.

Sarah giving Joel presents in the show
Joel and Sarah with the watch in the game

The Scene

There is a scene.. of Sarah dying. And the game, if you didn't know about it happening before you played the game, and if you didn't know what was going to happen in the show.. wow. POWERFUL.

The Joel and Sarah scene

Joel

Pedro Pascal is Joel. Wow, he is great here. He embodies Joel in so many ways, and he is a great adaptation of Joel. Can't wait to see more.

Pedro Pascal as Joel

Ellie

Bella Ramsey is Ellie, YES! From her first scene, which is different from the game and it is just great. The way she has that bite (no pun intended) towards her captors is awesome!

Bella Ramsey as Ellie

Review Score

Without a doubt 5/5. Oh boy, I'm so excited for more! Be on the lookout for more episode reviews.

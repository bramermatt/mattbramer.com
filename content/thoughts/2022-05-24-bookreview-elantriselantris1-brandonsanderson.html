Elantris (Elantris, #1) Book Review

Elantris (Elantris, #1) by Brandon Sanderson

My rating: 5 of 5 stars

Wow. I am amazed at this book. It took A LOT for me not to rate this at a 4/5. As I am not too much into the political story, but there is so much more to this book than just that. However, it takes about 400-450 pages to get into the best parts of this book. Even still, I can’t wait to reread this book after a few years. Elantris by Brandon Sanderson 5/5!

Towers of Midnight (The Wheel of Time, #13) Book Review

Towers of Midnight (The Wheel of Time, #13) by Robert Jordan

My rating: 4 of 5 stars

Written Review


Why yes, I THOROUGHLY ENJOYED THIS ENTRY! This one struggled a bit for me being a middle book on a trilogy. A lot was resolved, but there was a lot left open.. obviously as this penultimate novel in a 15 book series. We are not done yet, and there is still something big to come! And there was even some plots STARTED this book.

A satisfactory entry into the series. This one certainly separates itself as primarily written by Brandon Sanderson. There are friendly cameos from friends of the Wheel of Time community. There are beautiful one-liners. Great action. The dialogue between the characters is just so crisp. Oh this was a great, and I keep saying entry, because it is - it is an entry into the Wheel of Time!

I am ecstatic to start A Memory of Light next week! Book thirteen complete, what's left.. book fourteen and I've left the prequel New Spring for after the series to complete my first read-through of The Wheel of Time!

Spoiler Story Breakdown



Lan has gathered over 12,000 from the Borderlands and has raised his title Lord of the Seven Towers, Defender of the Wall of First Fires, Bearer of the Sword of the Thousand Lakes. We leave Lan charging into battle with over 150,000 Shadowspawn at Tarwain's Gap..

Moiraine oh wow, Moiraine is back! Moiraine was kept in the Aelfinn and Eelfinn world and they fed on her Power and she is weakend, but she has an ivory bracelet angreal. Mat, Thom, and Noal (Jain Farstrider) saved her from the snakes and foxes. Moiraine and Thom professed their love and he mentioned he would be her Warder.

Noal turns out to be Jain Farstrider, and he sacrificed himself to help save Moiraine.

Elayne was able to reconnect with Galad, Gawyn, Mat, Thom, Perrin, and... MORGASE. Elayne has also borrowed the foxhead ter'angreal from Mat and was able to create copies of the ter'angreal in exchange for help with Aludra's dragons. Elayne now has the throne of Cairhien.

Morgase married Tallanvor.

Aviendha goes to Rhuidean and goes through the columns and then goes a second time.. and sees the future of the Aiel. She has a plan to help sto pthat from happening.

Nakomi.. who is this?! She has showed up and disappeared after talking with Aviendha.

Nynaeve OH MY GOSH, HEALS MADNESS and passes her test for the shawl and takes Lan's bond from Myrelle.

Egwene believes Rand is mad, intending to break the Seals and she summons armies wtih the intent to stop him. Egwene also bonds Gawyn as Warder! Egwene faces Mesaana in TAR in the reflection of Tar Valon and Mesaana and the Black Ajah are there. Egwene breaks Mesaana's mind in a battle of wills.

Perrin trains with Hopper in the wolf dream (which I'll come back to this), and finds a strange wall.. turns out to be a dreamspike setup by Slayer/Isam/Lord Luc. Perrin also was judged by Morgase against Galad and the Whitecloaks - they considered Perrin a Darkfriend for killing some Whitecloaks. Morgase declared guilty and gave over the punishment to Galad. Perrin then saved Galad and the Whitecloaks from a Trolloc attack. Perrin and Hopper steal the dreamspike and end up destroying the dreamspike from Slayer. Hopper is killed. Perrin learns not to fear the wolf inside him and reunites with Noam, the wolfbrother. Oh, Perrin had a lot this book. Perrin crafted a Power-Wrought hammer with the help of Neald, the Asha'man, and Wise Ones and now the Asha'man might be able to make more Power-Wrought Weapons - the Aiel really want those! Perrin also made steward of the Two Rivers.

Graendal was the one who sent Slayer and the dreamspike to Perrin, and is now punished by Shaidar Haran.

Olver wins a game of Snakes and Foxes, and now plans on killing the Shaido who killed his father.

Caemlyn is being seiged by Shadowspawn from the Waygate in Caemlyn.

Mat killed the gholam.. still means there might be 5 left right? Mat, Thom, and Noal entered the Tower of Genjei and saved Moiraine. Mat had his eye removed by the Eelfinn to give Moiraine her freedom. Using his ashandarei to open a doorway out of the Tower of Ghenjei.

Rand comes down from Dragonmount, and is changed. His presence makes all things better. He visits Egwene and announces he will break the Seals of the DO at the Field of Merrilor. Rand apologizes to his friends, and goes to restore order in Bandar Eban, he also helps save Bashere and Ituralde. King Alsalam is also found alive. Rand also meets with the Borderland monarchs. He is ready to start the Last Battle.

Wolf Dream vs TAR okay, so these have to be the exact same thing right?! It isn't fully written out on the page, but also Perrin can see Egwene and they can communicate with each other. So this HAS THE BE THE SAME THING! I'm just waiting to see this on the page.

The Forsaken alive.. Damodred, Lanfear / Cyndane, Ishamael / Moridin, Moghedien, Graendal and Sammael (thinking still alive). Dead.. Asmodeon. Balefired.. Rahvin, Semirhage, Balthamel, Be'lal, Aginor.

The Seanchan now have Travelling from Suffa.. Elaida. They plan to use Traveling to attack the White Tower again!

The Black Tower something is happening here. Androl is building a bit of a resistance against Taim. There must be a dreamspike at the Black Tower because no one can make gateways in or out of the Black Tower. HMMM.

Lanfear in the epilogue Rand hears a woman crying and Rand shows up and it's Lanfear.. She is scared. I think Lanfear might be being saved by Rand?!?! Interesting.

Field of Merrilor is the place Rand will meet with monarchs, the Amyrlin Seat Egwene, Perrin and his forces, The Band of the Red Hand, the Whitecloaks, also known as the forces of the Light.

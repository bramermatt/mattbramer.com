Mistborn: The Final Empire (Mistborn, #1) Book Review

Mistborn: The Final Empire (Mistborn, #1) by Brandon Sanderson

My rating: 5 of 5 stars

Written Review
If you've never read Brandon Sanderson before, this is an incredible introduction! Amazing magic, amazing plot, amazing characters everything you'd want in a fantasy story.

This is an amazing book. This story is awesome and exciting, and really asks a lot of great questions. There are so many aspects of this book that are completely incredible.

If you've read my previous reviews, you'll know that I read a lot of other Brandon Sanderson books prior to reading this book. I've read most of the Stormlight Archive, Warbreaker, Elantris, and one day I'll have to do a ranking of my favorite Cosmere books!

I think Brandon Sanderson writes spectacular female lead characters, none are really damsels - but Vin in this book.. WOW!

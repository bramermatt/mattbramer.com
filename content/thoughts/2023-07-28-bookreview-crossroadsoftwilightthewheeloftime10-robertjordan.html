Crossroads of Twilight (The Wheel of Time, #10) Book Review

Crossroads of Twilight (The Wheel of Time, #10) by Robert Jordan

My rating: 2 of 5 stars

Oh wow, this book was hard to read. Boring and slow, and nothing really happened. The worst Wheel of Time book so far. I wanted this book to be so interesting and it really wasn't. The ending of book nine Winter's Heart, this had a lot to live up to and it fell very flat. Nothing really happened and that's why I struggled with this book. So many chapters were too long, and too boring, and nothing really projected to plot. I'm super excited to get to the next book. Wow.

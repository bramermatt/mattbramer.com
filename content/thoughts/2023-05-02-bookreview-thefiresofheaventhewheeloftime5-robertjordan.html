The Fires of Heaven (The Wheel of Time #5) Book Review

The Fires of Heaven (The Wheel of Time #5) by Robert Jordan

My rating: 5 of 5 stars

This was an amazing Wheel of Time Book! The stakes are raised higher and higher! This book was one of my favorite so far. Each book gets better and better, really. This world is blowing up (getting bigger) and I'm constantly impressed by Robert Jordan and his writting! WOW. SUCH A GOOD BOOK!

Thoughts on FOH
Good grief. This was an awesome book. I'll need to reread my notes and think about my thoughts for each book to decide, but this was an EPIC ending. I am impressed with how everyone has leveled up FOR SURE. I really think Rand is going mad.. maybe. I really appreciate LTT inside of Rand's head. I also really love the shifting of Rand and LTT in the Caemlyn Royale Palace. That was cool to read, because Rand was fighting to be full. So much from Forsaken.. this was such a good boook.

We got A LOT this book. Darkhounds, Forsaken, T'A'R', Lews Therin, Balefire, a CIRCUS! I loved this book.

Birgitte
I cannot believe we've got Birgitte in the waking world. AND ELAYNE HAS BONDED HER AS A WARDER?!

Nynaave
This was an amazing Nynaeve book. I was really glad to see what we saw from Nynaeve, with her Power and her ability to learn things and her wanting to be a Yellow (she really wants to Heal).

Elayne
I really liked Elayne this book. She is powerful and apparenlty she is going to do something with the a'dam.. I have no idea what's going on with that.

Thom
I'm glad Thom and Elayne are not going to try and be in a relationship. That was a little weird. I love the Thom / Gareth Bryne dynamic. Thom is always awesome.

The Circus
This was amazing and fun, I loved it.

Siuan
A deposded Siuan is not a weak Siuan.. SO GOOD.

Logain
Glory? Yet to be seen.

Rand, The Lord Dragon Reborn, Car'a'carn
Well, Rand, LTT, Rand is AWESOME. I am so excited to read more about Rand. I want to learn so much more about the Power and everything. THIS IS GREAT! Super powerful with the a'ngrael in his pocket, and getting really good about his use of the Power. Rand and Aviendha had sex. Whoa. That was pretty crazy when that happened.

Aviendha
I love Aviendha. I appreciate when she's actually telling Rand things - not when she's just brushing him off and being sort of rude to Rand for not knowing everything. But I look forward to Rand + Min + Elayne + Avienda. I'm also really interested in seeing what Elayne does when she finds out about the igloo.

Mat
I love Mat. He is so funny and awesome. I love that he is becoming this general and now has his own banner.. it was time he got his own.

Asmodean
I was so happy for Rand to have a teacher, and now I have no idea what Rand is going to do without Asmo!

Rahvin
Dead. I'm glad. I really hated what he was doing to Morgase - I'm really interested to know what happens with the Compulsion since Rahvin is dead.

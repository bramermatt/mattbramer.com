Brotherhood (Star Wars) Book Review

Brotherhood (Star Wars) by Mike Chen

My rating: 4 of 5 stars


Emotional Impact 5
I have so much love for Star Wars, and I’m just happy to have more. I enjoyed the story.

Characters 5
We got a seemingly small story here, but I was impressed with what I saw from the main cast.

Plot 3
Not my favorite Star Wars story. But an enjoyable story.

Dialogue / Prose 4
An easy read. I really enjoyed seeing the Force portrayed in a book. Also I love the Obi / Ani interactions.

Cinematography / World Building 4
This is held on the shoulders of the entirety of the Star Wars universe so that gives some to hold this up. But this story only had a few different backdrops, but they were well described.

Welcome to my site! I’m Matthew Bramer—a pastor, teacher, and follower of Jesus. I’m also a worship leader, husband, and dad who’s passionate about helping people grow in their understanding of the Christian faith. Through my writing and teaching—especially in my Before the Throne series—I explore Scripture, sound doctrine, and the beauty of the gospel in ways that are clear, honest, and rooted in God’s Word.

Alongside that, I offer reflections on books I’m reading and thoughtful commentary on current events—all with the goal of helping believers think biblically in a noisy and complex world. Whether you’re new to the faith or have walked with Jesus for years, you’re welcome here.

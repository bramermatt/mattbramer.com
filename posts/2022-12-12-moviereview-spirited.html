Spirited Movie Review

December 12, 2022

Movie Reviews

Trailer

Watch the trailer below, but man, if you don't want to be spoiled of any funny bits: don't watch the trailer! I'd recommend going into this movie with no knowledge of the premise. I did that and I loved it!

Official Spirited Trailer on YouTube

My Thoughts

This movie was amazing, hilarious, sad, smart, and a really good time. I'd recommend anyone with Apple TV+ watch this movie. This is a 2 hour movie, and it felt a little long, but that could've also been because we finished the movie at midnight. Other than that, an amazing movie and a must watch!

Spoilers..

Time to spoil! You've been warned.


This Movie is a Musical

I had no idea this movie was a musical! But it's so good too. The songs are funny and really good to listen to. Ryan and Will are really good singers, as is Octavia Spencer!

Spirited Movie Screenshot

Just Some Funny Scenes

Here are a few screenshots of some funny scenes..

Early in the Movie, Ryan Reynolds and Will Ferrell

Will is a ghost, I won't spoil too much.. and Ryan is an alive character and Ryan is talking on the phone here and it's just so funny to see these two acting so closely together.

Elf Scene

As we all know, Will Ferrell played Buddy the Elf in Elf, here we see a character dressed as Buddy the Elf.. I won't spoil, but it's very funny.

Will Ferrell

Will never disappoints, and he most certainly did not here either. Always so funny, and a really good actor when it counts.

Ryan Reynolds

Oh wow Ryan Reynolds... such a nice guy. I imagine he is a great guy in person, as I feel the same with Will Ferrell. But Ryan was incredible in this movie. So witty and funny. So lighthearted and so good when it needed to be.

Score

I'd give this a 5/5, a must watch! I'll probably rewatch this again, but not as much as I rewatched the Greatest Showman!

Good afternoon!

Written Review

I am currently reading, as of February 13, 2023, and I'm no where near being done - so this is going to be a review in progress! And below, I'm going to have notes as I read, by chapter. More than likely spoilers, so I'm going to keep those hidden by the box below. When I've finished my read, the Notes box will be moved below the spoiler box, probably past the score!

OKAY WOW. This is my favorite Wheel of Time book I've ever read. This is almost non-stop amazing, every single chapter. The video review I filmed first was 21 minutes long, and it could've been hours. I'm amazed by this book and amazed at the Wheel of Time and how Robert Jordan writes. This is incredible.

Spoilers

Just a bit of preamble here.. the way this review will be is a lot of different from others. I'm wrote notes on every single chapter that I read. Which is completely spoiler, so this is where the spoilers start.. read at your own risk!

Review Scores

I'm trying a new way of reviewing, by implementing a new way to review - using a format taken from 2toRamble! And that’s the system! Rate those 5 categories out of 10, average your score, and that’s your /10 Rambler Rating! You can rate each category to the nearest hundredth, tenth, whole number, or whatever you please.

This comes out to a total score of 5! An incredible book!

Emotional Impact 5
After many thousands of pages with these characters, we've loved these characters and this world, and this book is such a high point. Amazing and I'm so emotionally invested!
Characters 5
THIS IS A CHARACTER MASTERPIECE. I'm amazed at how Robert Jordan was able to create and write these characters.
Plot 5
This is amazing.. I can't stop saying that, but this is my favorite Wheel of Time book so far. And, this plot, I think, is the best plot of the series so far. It is so big and so important to the progression of the story! AMAZING!
Dialogue / Prose 5
Robert Jordan writes so beautifully and so powerfully. Dialogue here is strong.
Cinematography / World Building 5
This book has the best World Building of the Series and everything and everyone is leveling up. This story, four books in, is HUGE. I can't imagine there are 10 more books after this, I can't imagine this whole story being bigger than it is now!

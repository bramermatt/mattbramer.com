Star Wars: Aftermath Book Review

February 10, 2022

Video Review

Written Review

An engaging story of the New Republic and the Empire! The beginning, of course, is a slow build, meeting new characters with storied histories. Then, a metaphoric flip is switched and we are off to the races.

The second half of the book is a whirlwind of emotions, of triumphs, of defeats, of cliff hangers, all to a satisfying ending.

Where to next? Luckily there are two more books in this series!

Chuck Wendig, nice job!

P.S. I just "got back into" reading this year, and I started off with this book, and this book revitalized my love of reading. I have quite a large TBR(to be read) now, and that's some thanks to this book in particular.

P.P.S. If you're a Star Wars fan, this is a MUST READ!

My Problem

I've been having a problem with YouTube.com ever since YouTube Shorts became a reality..

YouTube Shorts have impeded on my YouTube subscriptions, when I feel that should not happen. When I go to select Subscriptions - I'd expect to see videos from my subscriptions, but I see videos and YouTube Shorts. Which makes sense, but I'd really like to be able to separate the Shorts from the Videos.. but I cannot.

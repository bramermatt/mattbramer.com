What is Not Another Darkfriend Podcast?

May 2, 2023

I have a podcast!

I have recently started a podcast about the Wheel of Time and my thoughts, theories, and predictions! It's slow going as I'm reading the series, and I think I'm much more comfortable on camera. But, I'm excited to start this and here we go!

Podcast Episode 0

Click the image and you'll be taken to the first episode of the podcast!

Goals

The plan is to record episodes after I read chapters and review the books in audio form. With a video that shows up on my Youtube Channel!

At the time of writing.. I've only recorded one episode and I have just finished The Fires of Heaven, which is book five. This series has 15 books... so I've got a ways to go, and that's part of the reason why I started this podcast!

Patreon

I've also got some ideas about starting a Patreon. Because there are some monthly fees that are required for keeping a podcast up and running. So, I might ask for some crowdfunding! We'll see. Thank you all!

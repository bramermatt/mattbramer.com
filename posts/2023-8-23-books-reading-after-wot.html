When Will I Finish WOT

My plan to finish the Wheel of Time is to finish before our daughter, Hazel, is born. She is due in early October! Our son, Everett (3), is very very excited to meet her.

This was a self-imposed timeline, but I think I'm going to be able to do it! I'm able to read at work, I work in IT, and that is super helpful! Otherwise, I wouldn't be able to finish these books in the time that I am finishing these books.

I'm halfway through Towers of Midnight, on page 545 to be exact, and there are 946 pages in the whole book. I should be able to finish this book Friday, if all goes well. There are extenuating circumstances everywhere, but this is what I'm looking at!

After Towers of Midnight

Towers of Midnight is book thirteen, there are fifteen books in the series including the prequel which I haven't read yet. So, after Towers of Midnight, I'll read A Memory of Light book fourteen. That's 920+ pages! And the final book in the series.. oh I'm so excited about that. Now, after that, I'll read the prequel New Spring! That's a solo Robert Jordan novella. Brandon Sanderson completed the series after Robert Jordan's passing in 2007.

And that'll be the final Wheel of TIme book that I'll read for the first time.. yes, that is so good. Typically, people like to start rereading The Wheel of Time after you've finished reading The Wheel of Time. I'm not going to do that.

Why I'm Not Rereading Right Now

I love the Wheel of Time, it is incredible. Oh, it is so so good. But, I'm also very excited to read some Brandon Sanderson, Stephen King, Rick Riordan, James S.A. Corey (the Expanse), and more on my bookshelf. I may very well do some more reading of the Wheel of Time in between reading other things, because I'm also going to try and buy all the hardcover editions of the books!

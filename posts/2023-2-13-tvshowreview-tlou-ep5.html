Last of Us in Review Reviews

Written Review

This was a great episode! We got so many things that I didn't expect, and it certainly delivered. The acting in this show is top notch, and I am really excited to see what happens next.

Spoiler Alert

No More Kathleen and Perry

This was a great ending to these characters, they were short-lived and that was good. I wasn't really resonating with them, but it

Perry and Kalthleen

Why No More Kathleen and Perry

Because a BLOATER SHOWED UP. HOLY COW! This scene was no joke! Amazing acting by the characters, amazing cinematography, just a cool scene in general with the ground caving in and the infected literally sprinting over each other. Wow.

Bloater

Sam and Henry

Oh, Sam and Henry. These boys were so good. They really showed their teamwork together in this episode and I loved the ASL aspect of their relationship - from the behind the scenes it seemed like they were really nice guys. But in the show, we saw their demise due to Sam's bite.. which Ellie tried to save with her blood (which I thought was interesting). But, they really were the stars of this episode for sure.

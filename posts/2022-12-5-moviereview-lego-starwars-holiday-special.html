Let's talk the trailer first.. DO NOT WATCH THE TRAILER IF YOU DON'T WANT TO BE SPOILED. I came into this movie completely blind and I will say the trailer spoils a majority of the enjoyment for sure. Please be warned.

Lego Star Wars Holiday Special Trailer

Review

This is an absolute delight to watch! I was looking for the original Star Wars Holiday Special, and I couldn't seem to find it on Disney+, but I'll keep looking.

If I'd have to give this a score, I'd say 5/5! This is a great Star Wars story. I didn't know what to expect when I started the movie, but this story takes you through the entire Skywalker Saga using a key to go to key points in the Star Wars Skywalker Saga inwhich we see important Master and Padawan moments. Great stuff!

I was able to watch this with my son, Everett, and we had an absolute blast! He really enjoyed it!

Lego

In the past I've been a huge fan of Lego Star Wars games, and most recently I've embarked on a journey to complete The Lego Star Wars: Skywalker Saga on Switch! So, I've been around a bit with Lego Star Wars, but I'd never watched a full Lego Movie, or the Lego Movie really. I was a bit skeptical, but it turned out to look really great and be a fun time!

Final Thoughts

There really isn't much to say, other than, if you're a Star Wars fan: you're going to love this!

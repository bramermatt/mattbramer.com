    • Tracked Ride

    Today I tracked my ride, from work to over the Big Four Bridge and back! Made in good time too. I'm feeling really good about ridding now. It's a lot of fun. Today I dealt with a lot of headwind which caused some slow down. I also had my chain slip again, and I need to get that looked about before my next ride.

    Screenshot from Apple's Fitness App

    Here are my stats, pretty good I think. I felt like i was going faster than normal. I'm practing some different riding styles like standing up to go faster. That seems to help.

    Next Ride

    I'm going to try a different route tomorrow, unless it rains. Monday should start my Bike Commuting To Work Officially!

Star Wars: Brotherhood Book Review

February 13, 2023

Star Wars: Brotherhood Book Cover
Reviews Book Reviews Star Wars in Review

Written Review

This was an awesome book, a lot of fun, and a really great Star Wars Story! This is only my second Star Wars book that I've read and it was a great time! It was a bit of a shorter book - 329 pages in the Hardback, but it was a good time, for sure. This was a story of.. "Cato Nemoidia", and it was a good succinct story, and I liked that. It wasn't too big or too long, and it was a delightful read for anyone who loves Star Wars!

Also, some very LAUGH OUT LOUD SCENES, like this about Sand... "See," he said to no one in particular, "no one ever believes me but it gets everywhere." I couldn't stop laughing about this.

Spoiler Alert

Obi-Wan

I love Obi-Wan, and I always have - and I felt Mike Chen did an incredible job here with the way Obi-Wan both spoke and acted.

Anakin

I also love Anakin, and his character here is a good encapsulation of Anakin's character. I love the scenes with Anakin and Mill, because it is Anakin being this character that hasn't had a Youngling or Padawan before and I think it was just great.

Mill

Mill was a lot of fun! Her take on everything was a good read. She is a good character to have as someone who is questioning everything, but not annoying. I really liked her character.

This Story of Cato Nemoidia

This was a good story, I thought. Interesting story of politics and Star Wars, always a good bet!

Score

This scored a total of 3.8 out of 5, which rounds up to 4/5! I really appreciated the story and I enjoyed my time with the book. It didn't absolutely blow me away, but I enjoyed it.

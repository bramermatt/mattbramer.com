What to Expect?

I read the entirety of the bible in 2021. I wrote down my notes as I read.

My first full read through of the Bible. Done in 239 days. List of numbers: 7.9 months, 239 days, 66 books, 1022 pages, February 8 to October 4. The NLT was an awesome read! Definitely made it easy to understand for me, being that I’ve been a believer for a while now. I’m excited to dive deeper with NASB and ESV study bibles. Not sure what I’m going to do next. Definitely swapping my NLT with my new NASB Bible in my backpack as an everyday carry! Woo! I did it!

I also filmed a few videos of a review of sorts for my readings. I'll add those when we get to the different testaments.

We'll start, with The Old Testament!

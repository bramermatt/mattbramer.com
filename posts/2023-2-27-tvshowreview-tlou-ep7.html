TLOU Ep. 7: "Left Behind" Review

February 27, 2023

Last of Us in Review Reviews

Written Review

Left Behind was a prequel game for the Last of Us, focused on Ellie! I never played it, but I have heard some amazing things about it! But, this episode was a delight to watch! Great chemistry between these two, for sure! This was so good.

Spoiler Alert

Ellie and Riley

These two are great! This was a very fun episode.. well fun might not be the best word for it. This is just great! These were great actors here. These were great scenes, wow!

Riley and Ellie - in game
Ellie and Riley

The Mall

This was a great set! Great color, great atmosphere. My only concern was they didn't really pay attention to their surroundings before it was too late. It was nice while it lasted, obviously. Also, terrifying when the lights are off.

Ellie and Riley Looking
Ellie looking at the Mall

From Game to Screen

From what I've seen from the Left Behind game, this is very close to the game. And for someone whose not played the game, this was a great episode as it was.

The Surprise

Again I'll say, these were great actors portraying this amazing story. But, whoa. THESE GIRLS DO NOT CARE ABOUT THEIR SURROUNDINGS. I have said this so many times this episode, this was incredible.

Don't Go

Well, I knew this was coming, and it was nice but whoa this was SCARY!

What's Next for Joel?

We see Ellie trying to take care of Joel when Joel wants Ellie to go, what's she going to do? Okay, well Ellie looks like she's got it!

Joel in bad shape

NEXT EPISODE PREVIEW

OMG. DAVID. ALSO TROY BAKER YES PLEASE. Okay, no more screaming.. wait THE DINER ON FIRE WE ARE GETTING THAT SCENE! YES!

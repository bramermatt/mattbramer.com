There is so much to watch

I'm truly struggling to watch everything that is on tv, to keep up with every series and franchise that I want to keep up with - it's tough. I had all these plans for multiple reviews of series and different things, but somedays I just sit and watch YouTube or read. I'm weighed down by the amount of shows to watch at once - but that's the joy of media right? Maybe.

Reading is My Favorite Though

I'm obsessed with reading, and I just can't stop reading to do anything else! It is so much fun to be apart of a fandom of people that love reading as well. I'm on Discord's for Podcasts about the Book Series that I'm reading..

I Must Read What's on My Book Shelf..

Before buying anymore books.

I know, crazy? Not crazy though. I have 20 books on my shelf that I haven't read, not counting the Biblical Theology books or Bibles. And books are expensive, at least the books I like.

Example: Brandon Sanderson Books

The Stormlight Archive are books that are over 1,000 pages in both paperback or hardback. And those are all around $25 depending on the book, as an example Edgedancer and Dawnshard are around 160 pages at $10.

So, as of right now (2022), there are 6 Stomlight Archive books. Let's do some math.

  • The Way of Kings: $25
  • Words of Radiance: $25
  • Edgedancer: $10
  • Oathbringer: $25
  • Dawnshard: $10
  • Rhythm of War: $25
  • Total of JUST STORMLIGHT SO FAR.. $120

$120 just for the Stormlight Archive. That's a lot of money, when you don't include all the other books on my current book shelf.

NO MORE BUYING NEW BOOKS...

At least for right now. I am going to work on reading all my unread books on my shelf before buying a new book. That's going to be difficult for me because now I'm addicted to buying books.

So let's see how this goes, not buying any new books..

Is it Possible?

Is it possible to buy a book and read it, without reading another book at that time?

Currently Reading

It's November 17, 2022 and I am reading Oathbringer by Brandon Sanderson! Book Three of the Stormlight Archive. You can see I still have Dawnshard and Rhythm of War to buy and read. But, with all the books on my shelf it might take some time before I get to those books.

What am I Going to Read Next?

I was going to buy Dawnshard by Brandon Sanderson (book 3.5 in the Stormlight Archive). But now, I just don't know.. I might go in order from left to right as I have them on the shelf. But we shall see.

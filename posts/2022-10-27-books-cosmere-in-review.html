A Cosmere Review Series?

You might've seen that I've been reading a lot of Brandon Sanderson's work lately. Brandon has written interconnected universe called the Cosmere! There are tons of books that Brandon has written in this universe. So many so, that I am going to do a Cosmere Review Series!

This is REAL!

Since the writing of this blog, this page exists: Here!

What is the Cosmere?

Brandon talks about it here
Here are only some of the Cosmere Books

Here is a link to Brandon's site about all his books: Cosmere Books

Current Stormlight Archive Review Series

I currently have a Stormlight Archive Series that has currently, as of 10.27.22, The Way of Kings Review and Words of Radiance Review.

This is going to transition to be the Cosmere Review Series. There will be separate sections for each specific Cosmere series, Stormlight Archive, Mistborn, Standalone, Collections, and even some Graphic Novels!

This is going to take quite some time to read all of these books, but there are still a lot of Cosmere books not written. And I.. cannot.. wait!

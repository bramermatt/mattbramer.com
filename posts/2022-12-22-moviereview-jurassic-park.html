Jurassic Park Movie Review

December 22, 2022

Movie Reviews

My Thoughts

First off, I’ve never seen Jurassic park before but wow! This movie is great!

The Setup

This movie is doing so great setting up everything to make sense of this world and why this is.

Music

What a theme! Amazing!

Acting

I’m actually very surprised, this has been an incredible movie. Everyone on the cast really brought some great acting to the screen. And surprisingly this was a fairly small cast, I'm sure that changes when future movies come out..

The Characters in Jurassic Park Movie

Power Station Scene

Just superb. Tension, great acting. Very scary.

The Dinosaurs

Love the practical effects!

The T-Rex Scene..
The Braciosaurus Scene..

Final Thoughts & Score

My initial reason for watching this movie was to see if my two year old son, Everett, would be able to watch it. Turns out, he probably can't a little scary and people are literally eaten by dinosaurs.. so... Really, I think I found a movie that is quite possibly one of the best movies I've ever seen and I look forward to watching more of the Jurassic movies and maybe even the Jurassic Park book! This movie was also very tense! I honestly had never seen this movie before so I didn't know what to expect, but I had a great time! My Score is, without a doubt, a 5/5!

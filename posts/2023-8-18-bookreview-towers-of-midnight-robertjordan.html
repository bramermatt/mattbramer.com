Review Scores

I will say, I was very excited for this book, and I thoroughly enjoyed it - as I said. However, I give this a 4/5 for my scale of enjoyment. I really enjoyed the dialogue and the plot, and the world-building of course, but this book felt held back.

Obviously it had to be, being the second to last book, but there were certain parts of the story that I'll leave out in case you haven't read the book - I wasn't as emotionally satisfied. I wanted to be, but I couldn't be. There are still plotlines that drag down my enjoyment. I want to enjoy them, but there are characters and people groups that aren't too very important to the story.

That doesn't mean I don't love this book, I certainly do. But, after all of that, 4/5.

Let's see how A Memory of Light finishes the series, and then New Spring after that!

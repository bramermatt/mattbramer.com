My Hair

There is just something about my hair, I just really enjoy my hair. My hair has always been a bit of a defining feature in my life. I'm not sure what it is, but as I've gotten older I've been working on my hair and my opinion, making more of an effort to improve my style.

My wife will tell you that my style used to be better, and that's probably true. Now, I worry more about comfort than style in my clothing choices.. I've been wearing the same shoes or shoe brand for the past three years.

2022

2022 has seen my hair the longest it's probably ever been. I'm really trying to grow it out, and I don't have a definitive answer on why I'm doing that. Pictured below you'll see two pictures of my hair, one from my most recent haircut MAY 7 2022 and today DECEMBER 1 2022!

That's probably the longest it's been between haircuts! It's been a lot of fun growing my hair out, I've been trying to train my hair to go straight back! Again, I don't have a goal other than wanting to be able to tie up my hair behind my head, in a bun. I love my long hair but I don't like it in my face. That's the point of the tie up!

TLOU Ep. 6: "Kin" Review

February 20, 2023

Last of Us in Review Reviews

Written Review

YES! This show, first off, is beautiful. This episode, is a good episode! Gotta move the story along and this has done a great job at that! I loved the characters in the beginning, they were funny and what a great scene.

I sometimes struggle with episodes that come out weekly - considering this series. Sometimes I just want all at once, and I lose a bit of interest if not every episode is outstanding. I love this show, and I really love the story - I'll keep updated as we get close to the end of the season.

Spoiler Alert

TOMMY!

He's alive and he's here! However, I think Tommy is lying! Also, Tommy is going to be a dad, that's nice.

I really like this Tommy portrayal, and he is a great brother for Joel here. I like the community that he's setup and it seems safe.

Not All Bad

"A bad reputation doesn't mean you're always bad.. not always, at least." This was a great line!

So How's Tess?

"She's alright, she's fine." That's interesting why Joel isn't telling Tommy all things.

Tommy and Joel

What Is Going On With Joel

My first thought was a panic attack, but we've heard from Joel that it is his heart - uh oh. Also, "come dawn, we're going our seperate ways.. no you aren't.".

Joel

ELLIE

Wow, this is just a great adaptation of the game, FOR SURE. So much of this new way to see the story and small nods to the game and other that are more big nods. Also, I can clearly see Ellie loves Joel and Joel loves Ellie.

The Diva Cup

Okay, I liked that scene.. Ellie will be needing to know about these things soon, and this is a bit of a reusable option.

Sarah was Joel's Daughter

A great scene, wow. These are just a great bit of scenes put together - I like this episode. And Joel's "don't" and "you have no idea what loss is". and Ellie "don't tell me that I'd be safe with anyone else, because I'd just be more scared without you".

The Movie Scene

THIS IS SCARY. I am terrified of just one infected showing up to kill all those kids - but that could be from all that Walking Dead I've seen. A little PTSD from before.

Ellie and Joel

Joel and Ellie

I love their chemistry!

The Bat

HOLY COW, I did not expect that, wow. Also, the neck snap by Joel - yeesh. And now... Ellie just let it all out "Joel, I can't do this without you".

Joel Passed Out

What is NEXT?!

Here we go, I really enjoyed the next parts of the game - let's see how that plays out here.

NEXT EPISODE PREVIEW

OH WHOA - LEFT BEHIND?! So, there is a bit of a Last of Us Prequel smaller game called Last of Us: Left Behind and it's about Ellie and her friend Riley.. and it looks like that is what's the next episode.

Score

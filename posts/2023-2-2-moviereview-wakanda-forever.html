Black Panther: Wakanda Forever Review

February 2, 2023

Movie Reviews

Written Review

The Black Panther Movie has done it again... Another great story of a villain with a great backstory - and great showing in this movie. Also, thankful for Disney+ for having this movie to watch with our subscription.

Shuri

I also enjoyed her character "what are those!", and so much more. She is really coming into her own here.

Letitia Wright as Shuri

Namor

Well color me impressed. This character blew me a away, and I thought Killmonger was a great villain.

Tenoch Huerta as Namor

T'Challa

This movie had to tell the story of how his character died as well, and I think this was a good send off for the character.

Chadwick Bowsman from Black Panther

Before You Proceed

There is a particular aspect to spoilers that I prefer to make known to you.

If you don't want to see the spoilers, you won't see them. There is a clear break when spoilers begin and they are typically separated by a RED box with the words "Spoilers".

If you'd prefer not to read them, don't! More power to you. But, thank you for reading as you do.

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever Review

February 2, 2023

Spoilers

The Invader Scene

Holy cow, I loved this scene, the action was great and believable. I really can believe this sonic thing that is happening.. not sure how to explain it exactly - but the movie does it well!

Namor just comes up out of the water.. "the fishman"!

M'Baku

Oh so good, always funny, really didn't get much screen time in this movie. But well worth every time he was on screen. "I'm here because I promised your brother that I would provide you with council and protection". SO GOOD.

Winston Duke as M'Baku

Namor

It just seems he can't die (as I'm writing this I'm only 93 minutes in). "Bury your dead, mourn your losses, you are queen now." POWERFUL...

Namor in Full Garb

The Queen - Ramonda

This movie is just plain extraordinary. The Queen was an absolute powerhouse when it comes to acting and this story. Just incredible. Her death hit hard.

Angela Bassett as Queen Ramonda

The Queen - Shuri

"my heart was buried with her...." SO GOOD. "The Black Panther has never failed Wakanda". Boy I'm about to cry right now, this is so so good. "I built you a new spear", these lines are superb. "And what I want is Namor DEAD, and you're going to help me".

Letitia Wright

"I think you're going to need a suit"

THE BLACK PANTHER. SO MUCH POWER WOW WOW WOW. So GOOD! "She has done it; The Black Panther LIVES!" The same line that T'Challa said too.. here we go again *crying*. "NO, I am the Black Panther and I am here for Retribution". "WAKANDA FOREVER".

Letitia Wright as The Black Panther

"The Scientist" - Riri

She was fun. "For the queen", nice. Really digging the Iron Man Suits, although we might need to come up with a new name.. maybe.

Dominique Thorne as Riri

MCU

Also, I'd say this movie has parts of all the MCU movies put together - what do I mean by that?

Wakanda Forever in the MCU

So, this story is a completely seperate story from all other MCU movies because it has no cameos and such; but this has a lot of real Iron Man 1 vibes - I mean we even have an Iron Man suit! And.. the T'Challa wall was a direct copy of Tony's death mural - okay before I found the picture of the Iron Man death mural, I thought it was of actually Tony.. the MANDELA EFFECT!

Chadwick Bowsman Mural
Iron Man Mural

Griot

Shuri's AI.. I have always wanted my own personal AI that I developed, I don't know that I ever will, but I want to!

Aspect Ratios

I wonder why the decision was made to shoot the two scenes - at least in timeline order going back and forth, being two different aspect ratios? Obviously the wider aspect ratio is more heroic for Namor and Shuri, but it is a bit jaring going back and forth so quickly; maybe that's just me. This does not take away from the movie in any capacity!

Example of Aspect Ratios in Wakanda Forever

T'Challa's Sendoff

Well get the tissues ready.. absolute perfection. Silence as we see Chadwick.. just perfect.

Toussaint

I cannot.. tears.

Review Score

The Marvel Cinematic Universe is back with this outing. An absolutely extraordinary 5 out of 5! There is something to be said about the characters that we've grown to love after a literal 30 movies - and as we see them again and again we love them more.

Musical Score

An absolute banger from start to finish.
